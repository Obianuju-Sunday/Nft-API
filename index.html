How to Create and Sell your NFTs

Setup your Wallet

Connect wallet of choice to Blaq.io
by clicking the profile icon at the
top right corner. Learn about the
wallets we support.

Create your Collection

Go to My Collections and set up
your collection. Add social links, a
description, profile & banner
images, and set a sales fee.

Add your NFTs

Upload your work (image, video,
audio, or 3D art), add a title and
description, and customize your NFTs
with properties, stats, and content.

List them for sale

Choose between auctions, fixed-price
listings, and declining-price listings.
You choose how you want to sell your
NFTs

Buy your Favorite NFT
and Sell your own NFTs too

Verify your NFT contract and build your custom store front.
We do our best to help you succeed.
